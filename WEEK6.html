What is a 'fully digital enterprise'?

A fully digital enterprise refers to an organization that extensively utilizes digital technologies, such as the internet, cloud computing, big data analytics, artificial intelligence, and other digital tools, across its entire value chain. It means that the organization's core operations, processes, and interactions are predominantly conducted in a digital format, with minimal reliance on physical, paper-based systems.
