GUEST LECTURE

This seminar was an important seminar organized by Instructor Beran. The seminar was presented by Ali Ulker and the topic was Arduino and its applications.

This seminar coincided with when we were just starting to learn about IoT. The main topic of my seminar was Arduino and its applications.Ali Ulker, who has a high level of knowledge on this subject, gave us a very instructive seminar.

In the beginning he told us what Arduino is.He said that Arduino is an open-source hardware and software company, project and user community that designs and manufactures single-board microcontrollers and microcontroller kits for building digital devices.

Then he talked about the projects that can be done with Arduino and the two examples he gave were traffic lights and water\rain sensors. and with that we started talking about the components of Arduino the most important components were JUMPER CABLES,LEDS,BREADBOARD,USB CABLE,RESISTANCE ,ARDUINO UNO Then Mr Ali said that we can try 2 small projects there. these two projects were water/rain sensor and turn on led lights with using button. We had the opportunity to try 2 small projects there, using all the components that he physically showed us.

The topics we talked about towards the end of the seminar were the programming language Arduino uses, which Arduino programs I can use, and where to buy the component sets shown to us. Mr. Ali finished the seminar with our questions.

My feelings about this seminar were very effective for us because it was much more effective when we tried and saw the subjects we saw theoretically in the course in practice.
