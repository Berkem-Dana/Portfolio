The subject of this activity was Netcraft. What was Netcraft?
Netcraft allows us to access information such as the operating system where the IP address or domain name you are querying is used, kernel information and even the software that the site is used in.

This is Whois Lookup, a site that does the same as Netcraft.

In this photo, we are querying the school's website and as you can see, it divides the Background into categories such as primary language, Netcraft Risk Rating.
In the network section, we see the domain owner, netblock owner, hosting country and IP address details.




And finally, in the Ip delegation section, we see and examine the full details of the IP address, we see the IP address change and Ip range in different countries.
