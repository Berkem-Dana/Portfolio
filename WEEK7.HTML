OWASP Top 10

OWASP(Oen Web Application Security Project) is a non-profit organization whose work is focused on improving software security.It finds most dangerous security threats to web applications and rank them according to their frequency of occurence and severity of impact.

IEEE Top 10

There are almost no sources of information on the Internet regarding IEEE Top 10 vulnerabilities. but I will try to convey what I know as much as research and learning.

IEEE Top 10

The IEEE Top 10, provides general secure design principles applicable to a broader range of software systems beyond web applications. It emphasizes secure design practices that can be applied to various software domains.
The IEEE Top 10 does not provide an exhaustive list of specific vulnerabilities. Instead, it focuses on general secure design principles that address a wide range of potential vulnerabilities. These principles guide developers in designing software systems with security in mind, rather than providing an explicit list of vulnerabilities.

Conclusion

In summary, the OWASP Top 10 and IEEE Top 10 lists serve different purposes and target different audiences. OWASP Top 10 provides a specific focus on web application vulnerabilities, while the IEEE Top 10 offers general principles for secure software design applicable to a broader range of software systems. Both sets of guidelines play a crucial role in promoting secure software development practices and should be considered in the context of specific development projects.
